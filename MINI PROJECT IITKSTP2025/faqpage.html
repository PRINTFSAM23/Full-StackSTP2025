Frequently Asked Questions

1. What kind of travel packages do you offer? We offer customized travel packages for solo travelers, couples, families, and groups. Packages include cultural tours, adventure trips, heritage experiences, and more.
2. Can I customize my itinerary? Absolutely! We specialize in personalized travel plans. You can modify your itinerary based on your preferences, budget, and travel goals.
3. How can I book a trip with Explore India? You can book directly through our website or contact our support team via email or phone. We’ll guide you through the booking process.
4. Is it safe to travel with Explore India? Your safety is our top priority. We partner with certified guides, verified accommodations, and follow all regional safety protocols.
5. Do you provide multilingual guides? Yes, we offer multilingual guide options in major languages such as English, Hindi, Tamil, French, and Spanish (based on location and availability).
6. What is your cancellation policy? You can cancel up to 7 days before your trip for a full refund. Cancellations within 7 days may incur a partial charge depending on arrangements already made.
7. Do you offer support during the trip? Yes, we provide 24/7 customer support to assist you throughout your journey, whether it’s a travel delay, emergency, or a change in plans.
